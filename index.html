About me

Hello! Welcome to my page. My name is Christopher J Guzman and I am pursuing a career as a web developer. My journey began with the pinball machine game on Windows XP. I kept asking myself, “What goes into creating a program like this? Where do they begin? How can it be made better?” My curiosity just kept growing. Then one day, a neighbor answered my question: “It’s all about code! and lots of it!” Intrigued, I researched about coding and realized that it was exactly what I wanted to pursue. Since then, I have gained an excellent understanding of basic HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT/JQUERY,NODE, Mysql and express fundamentals. I have grown into an efficient programmer who is able to adapt when faced with unexpected situations and who will always take opportunities to broaden my knowledge about a variety of frameworks.
